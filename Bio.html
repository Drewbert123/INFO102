Who am I?

(Yes, this is my dog)

Hey, I'm Drew. This website is meant to be a portfolio of sorts for the class I'm in, Informatics 102. At the time of writing this (2016) I'm a freshman at the University of Washington (go seattle!). Some of my personal hobbies include rock climbing, soccer, playing an assortment of video games, and handicrafting (woodworking, metalworking). Currently I'm trying to get a bachelors in Electrical Engineering at the University of Washington. I mostly want to help build/integrate circuits into vehicles. My dream job would be stress testing(trying to break) military vehicles and making sure the internal circuitry doesn't break, I want to do this since I think everybody has a little bit of a pyromaniac inside them. I'm born and raised in Washington and I absolutely love it here, and currently I wouldn't mind living here the rest of my life, since the Pacific NorthWest is awesome!
